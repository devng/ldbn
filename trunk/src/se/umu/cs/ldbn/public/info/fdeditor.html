FD Editor

The FD editor window allows users to define FDs by giving the left-hand side (LHS) and right-hand side (RHS) attributes of an FD.
Attributes must be separated by commas or white space, and they must be present in the "Given attributes" section, otherwise they will not be included in the FD.
The editor supports also Drag and Drop. User can drag any attributes or FDs and drop them in the text areas of the widget.

Set/Add button

Creates an FD from the attributes given in the two text boxes. Note that the dialog window will not close after pressing this button, thus users can further define FDs.

Clear button

Clears the text boxes from any given text inside.

Close button

Closes the dialog window.

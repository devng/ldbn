User login/registration

In this section you can login with your account, register a new one or use some additional help in order to obtain new password or a new registration email.
You only need an account, if you are going to post messages or create new assignments

Login

Here you can login with your account.
Please note, that for security reasons we only transmit a md5 hash of your password and not the actual password over the net, thus no one can obtain your real password.

Register

Here you can register a new LDBN account.
You need an account in order to post messages for each assignment or to create a new assignments.
After the account has been created, a registration mail has been sent out, so please check your mail and eventually your spam folder.

Help

Here you can obtain a new password in case You forgot the old one.
You can also resend the activation email of Your account, if You did not received any email.

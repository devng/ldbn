User login/registration

In this section registered users can login with their account. In addition, unregistered users can register or request a new registration email.
One only need an account, if one is going to post messages or create new assignments

Login

Here you can login with your account.
Please note that for security reasons we only transmit an MD5 hash of your password and not the actual password over the Internet, thus no one can obtain your real password.

Register

Here you can register a new LDBN account.
You need an account in order to post messages for each assignment or to create a new assignments.
After the account has been created, a registration mail has been sent out, so please check your mail and eventually your spam folder.

Help

Here you can obtain a new password in case you forgot the old one.
In case you did not received any email, you can also request a new activation email for your account.
